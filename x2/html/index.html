About us

We are Ellectric Callboy , a band born from the love for high-energy music and a desire to break boundaries. Since forming in 2010, we’ve carved our own path in the metalcore and electronic scene, blending heavy breakdowns with infectious synths and catchy choruses. What began as a fun, experimental project has turned into a movement, connecting with fans around the world who love that we don’t take ourselves too seriously but always bring 100% to our sound.

In recent years, we've seen our impact grow, especially with tracks like "Pump It" and our cover of "Everytime We Touch." With "Pump It," we embraced the energy of the gym, mixing intense metalcore with an upbeat, almost absurd workout vibe. Our take on "Everytime We Touch" gave new life to a classic, showing how we can bring nostalgic pop hits into our world of metal and electronic fusion. These tracks have helped us stand out, sparking a strong connection with our audience.

Biography

Electric Callboy is a German electronicore band formed in 2010 in Castrop-Rauxel. Originally known as Eskimo Callboy, they blended metalcore, post-hardcore, and electronic dance music (EDM) to create their unique sound. Their early releases, including Bury Me in Vegas (2012) and We Are the Mess (2014), quickly earned them a strong following with their wild party anthem style.

In 2020, the band introduced Nico Sallach as their new vocalist, following the departure of founding member Sebastian “Sushi” Biesler. Nico, formerly of To the Rats and Wolves, brought fresh energy, leading to the release of successful singles like "Hypa Hypa," which marked a creative turning point and expanded their fan base. His addition revitalized the band's sound and live performances.

In 2022, they officially changed their name from Eskimo Callboy to Electric Callboy to avoid any culturally insensitive connotations. This name change reflected their evolving public image while maintaining their energetic, playful spirit, helping them embrace a growing modern audience. The rebranding signaled a fresh chapter while staying true to their core essence.

Their latest album, TEKKNO (2022), cemented their status in the metalcore scene with its powerful blend of heavy breakdowns and catchy electronic beats. Featuring hit singles like "Pump It" and "Spaceman," the album was praised for pushing genre boundaries and capturing the energy of their live shows, solidifying their place as one of metalcore’s most innovative acts.
